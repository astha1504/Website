ABOUT US

Welcome to the Allenhouse Environment Club, where passion meets purpose in protecting our planet. We are a community of eco-conscious individuals dedicated to fostering sustainability, spreading awareness, and driving impactful environmental initiatives.

Through collaborative projects, educational workshops, and campus-wide campaigns, we aim to inspire meaningful change and promote green practices. Whether it's tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, or advocating for renewable energy solutions, we work tirelessly to make a difference.

Our club conducts different activities for its members throughout the year at the college, intercollegiate, and zonal levels. Join us as we strive for a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future for all.

Our Mission

At Jivita, we believe in action, impact, and innovation. Our mission is to drive sustainability through fun, engaging, and impactful initiatives. Join us as we make the world a little greener, cleaner, and better for future generations!

Organize Cleanliness Drives

Trash belongs in the bin, not on the streets! Our cleanliness drives bring together passionate volunteers to sweep away waste and keep our surroundings fresh.

Plant More Trees

One tree at a time, we're turning barren lands into lush green spaces. More trees = cleaner air, cooler climate, and happy wildlife!

Promote Waste Segregation

Did you know? Proper waste segregation can reduce 70% of landfill waste! Learn how to separate recyclables from trash in a fun and easy way.

Raise Water Conservation Awareness

Every drop counts! From fixing leaks to rainwater harvesting, we advocate for smarter water use to ensure no drop goes to waste.

Support Renewable Energy

Say goodbye to fossil fuels! We promote solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources to power a cleaner tomorrow.

Encourage Eco-Friendly Projects

Innovation meets sustainability! We encourage students to create solutions like compost bins, upcycled furniture, and eco-friendly packaging.
